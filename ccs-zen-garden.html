The Road to Enlightenment

Littering a dark and dreary road lay the past relics of browser-specific tags, incompatible DOMs, broken CCS support, and abandoned browser.

We must clear the mind of the past. Web enlightenment has been achieved thanks to the tireless efforts of folk like the W#C, WaSP, and the major browser creators.

The CSS Zen Garden invites you to relax and meditate on the important lessons of the masters. Begin to see with clarity. Learn to use the time-honred techniques in new and invigorating fashion. Become one with the web.
